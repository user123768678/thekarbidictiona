what is this site for?

This is a Karbi Dictionary site. you can search for words will you find difficult to understand or do not know the meaning of. there are over thousend words collection which have provided meaning. just look for the words and probably you will find the meaning.

How to use this website?

To find the meaning for the word, just click on the letter the word start with and look for the meaning. sometime you may not find the word. But keep patient, our volenteer are trying very hard to provided almost all the word use in karbi language. you also can contribute to this finding by becomeing word finder or editer. Headover to contribution section to know more.

Learn more about how to become word finder or editer.

Who main maintain this site?

This site is of non profit community based platform, which mean the site is maintain by volenteers and contributers. All the work on 'The Karbi Dictionary' is maintain by 'The Karbi Heritage Initiative' A community run organisation. Anyone can support the organisation by simply donating or become part of it.
